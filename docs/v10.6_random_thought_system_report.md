## Objective

Establish the random thought system — small, unbidden mental events that drift through consciousness. Random thoughts are NOT stories, dialogues, or LLM outputs. They are the small ripples of inner experience.

---

## Why Random Thought Is Not Story

Human consciousness is not continuous rational reasoning. Much of the time, a person just thinks something — briefly:

- "why didn't she reply?"
- "rain again"
- "maybe I should sleep"
- "phone light"

These are not plots. They don't have beginning, middle, end. They flash and fade. CharacterOS should acknowledge this: the mind drifts, memories surface, worries appear, desires leave shadows.

Random Thought is the small ripple in continuous life, not the narrative.

## Architecture

### RandomThoughtKind (9 kinds)

```
memory_echo | worry | desire_shadow | sensory_fragment | self_talk |
question | image | urge | nothing
```

`nothing` is a valid thought — sometimes the mind is simply quiet.

| Field | Source |
|---|---|
| `boredom`, `daydreamingTendency`, `restlessness` | BoredomExpansionState |

| `sleepResidue` | SleepWakeState (higher when waking/drowsy) |

### Generation Rules

**Kind selection** is weighted by context + source types:

- **Stress high** → worry, self_talk
- **Loneliness high** → desire_shadow, self_talk, memory_echo
- **Boredom/daydreaming high** → image, question, self_talk
- **Curiosity high** → question
- **Fatigue high** → sensory_fragment, nothing (suppresses question, worry)
- **Memory sources** → memory_echo
- **Dream sources** → image, sensory_fragment
- **Need sources** → urge, desire_shadow
- **Inspiration seed sources** → question, image

**Generation pressure** below 0.15 or no sources → `nothing`.

**Intensity** from emotional charge + stress + loneliness + boredom.  
**Clarity** from curiosity + daydreaming, reduced by fatigue + stress.  
**Action potential** varies by kind: urge > desire_shadow > question > self_talk > worry > memory_echo > image/sensory_fragment > nothing.

### Phrase Generation

Template-based, filled with source words or pre-defined fillers. Examples:
- `memory_echo`: "that time when {mem}", "{mem} again"
- `worry`: "what if {w}?", "maybe {w}"
- `desire_shadow`: "wish I could {d}", "if only {d}"
- `image`: "{img} light", "{img} again"
- `nothing`: "nothing in particular", "a pause", "blank"
