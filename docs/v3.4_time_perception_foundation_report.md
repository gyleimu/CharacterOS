# CharacterOS V3.4 Time Perception Foundation Report

## Version

```text
CharacterOS V3.4 Time Perception Foundation
```

## Goal

V3.4 introduces subjective time.

The goal is to represent that characters do not live in clock time only.

They live in interpreted time.

## Core Idea

```text
objective time != subjective time
```

Waiting, fear and loneliness can stretch time.

Joy, absorption and reward can compress time.

## Meaning

CharacterOS can now represent:

```text
three days of waiting may not feel like three days
joy may make time disappear
loneliness may make time heavy
```

This brings the system closer to a subjective world model rather than objective event accounting.
